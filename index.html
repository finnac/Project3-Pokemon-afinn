Welcome to Sinnoh!

Developed by Ashley Finn, Shay Kleeh, and Mary Meineke

Pokemon Diamond and Pearl is the 10th season of the Pokemon anime series, airing from April 20, 2007, to March 22, 2008, in the USA. The main characters include Ash Ketchum, Pikachu, Dawn, and Brock. It falls under the adventure, fantasy, and action genres. Following this season, "Pokemon: Diamond and Pearl: Battle Dimension" (Season 11) aired from April 12, 2008, to February 7, 2009, and "Pokemon: Diamond and Pearl: Galactic Battles" (Season 12) aired from February 13, 2009, to November 20, 2010, continuing the journey of Ash, Pikachu, Dawn, and Brock through the Sinnoh region. They battle trainers, capture Pokemon, and thwart the plans of the villainous Team Galactic. The selection of main characters for this project was based on two categories: line prevalence and memorability, aiming to feature prominent characters like Ash, Pikachu, Dawn, Brock, and the friends and foes they run into along their adventures.
